About me

As a Student

Devon is in the final Summer of his Bachelor of Science (BSc) in Cognitive Systems at the University of British Columbia, an interdisciplinary program involving the departments of Computer Science, Psychology, Linguistics, and Philosophy. Devon is in the Brain and Cognition specialization of the program, with a focus on Computer Science – he believes the intelligent design of any system should be grounded in the understanding of what makes its predecessor successful, whether it be natural or artificial. He is currently working as a co-pilot at UBC’s Visual Cognition lab, working as part of their coding team investigating human perception of correlation.

Outside school

Devon is also a competitive athlete, having placed 2nd at the 2017 International Dragonboat World Cup and 4th at the 2018 Club Crew World Championships. When he isn't training on thewater, Devon can usually be found enjoying the outdoors in some other way, escaping on hikes and camping trips whenever he is able to. He likes reading, spending time with friends, and supporting the local brewery scene.
